# everyone-ever

A live visualization of every human who has ever lived.

A "machine" on the left drops one baby every 8 seconds onto a conveyor belt; the conveyor delivers each newborn to an hourglass on the right (after the Max Roser / Our World in Data original). The upper bulb is the ~8.1 billion alive today; the lower bulb is the ~109 billion who have ever died; sand trickles continuously through the neck at the world's real death rate.

Live: https://everyone-ever.vercel.app

Each grain ≈ 10,000,000 people.
Each viz-baby ≈ 34 real births (world rate ≈ 4.3 births/sec, slowed for hypnotic effect).

Single-file static experiment. Open `index.html` directly or deploy as static.
